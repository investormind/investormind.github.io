Is saving worth it?

If we have a look at the topic of saving, we will notice that there are mainly two groups of views out there:
➙ According to the first view, saving is worthless. After all, you end up sacrifycing living a quality life for so little.
➙ The other view is the complete opposite. It stands that every penny is worth saving. It does not matter how much time and effort you will need to dedicate to save that penny, because at some point it will accumulate to a much bigger sum.
Which view is better?
As with everything, a mix of both is what you should aim at. This is what we refer to as "Smart saving".
